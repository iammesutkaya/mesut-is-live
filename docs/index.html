App Overview

LiveSticky is a secure, automated community utility built on the Reddit Developer Platform (Devvit). The Bot monitors the livestreaming status of a designated Twitch channel and handles submissions (creating and stickying live posts) on the community's subreddit to keep everyone perfectly in sync with stream events.
